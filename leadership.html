Flexibility, Constractiveness and Honesty

Flexibility: Be open to changes and adapt to evolving circumstances. Problem-Solving: Contribute to problem-solving efforts and suggest solutions. Constructive Feedback: Constructiveness: Provide feedback in a constructive and positive manner. Receptivity: Be open to receiving feedback and use it to improve. Leadership: Honesty: Be honest and transparent in your actions and communication. These expectations help create a positive and productive group dynamic, ensuring that individuals contribute effectively and work together harmoniously to achieve the group's objectives.
